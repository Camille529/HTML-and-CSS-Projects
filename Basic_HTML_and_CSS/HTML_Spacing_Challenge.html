Shoyo Hinata

There are many teams in the Haikyuu anime.
The main team the show follows is Karasuno,
which entails the main character Shoyo Hinata.
There are other characters as well,
but Shoyo's journey is the main focus.

Timeskip Shoyo Hinata

In the managa, there is a timeskip.
Essentially, it shows where all the characters currently are.
For Shoyo, they show him having graduated from High School,
training with a team in Brazil, and then playing for MSBY Black Jackals.
He also played for the Japan National Mens Volleyball Team.

As a first-year, Shoyo Hinata's jersey is # 10. As a third-year, his jersey is # 5.
